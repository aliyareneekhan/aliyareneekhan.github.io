The Gabii Project

Used ArcGIS to map an archaeological site which is located in Gabii, Italy. Worked with 3D Coat, PhotoScan, and the Unity Gaming environment to turn photographs taken of the site into a user-friendly, three-dimensional, interactive virtual representation. Traveled to Gabii, Italy and received direct training in photogrammetry, 3D modeling production, and archaeological topography. The published 3D environment will greatly influence how archaeological information is recorded by preserving a visual representation of the artifacts and structures in their original contexts.
